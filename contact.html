Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you contact us

How do I download ChinaGo?

ChinaGo is available on the App Store for iOS devices. Search for "ChinaGo" or click any download link on our website.

Is ChinaGo free to use?

Yes, ChinaGo is free to download and use. All core features including AI assistance, translation, and navigation are included at no cost.

Does ChinaGo work offline?

Essential features like basic translation and saved guides work offline, but AI assistance and real-time navigation require an internet connection.

What iOS version is required?

ChinaGo requires iOS 15.0 or later and works best on iPhone 8 or newer devices.

How do I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs through the app's feedback feature or by emailing help@chinago-ios.app with details about the issue.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from users. Send your feature requests to feedback@chinago-ios.app or use the contact form above.
